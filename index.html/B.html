In order to know the membership that suits you and the exercises that you

need to get a strong and healthy body, we advise you to know the body mass index.

The Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on weight and height. It is calculated by dividing the weight in kilograms by the square of the height in meters. The resulting value is used to classify individuals as underweight, normal weight, or overweight. The World Health Organization provides BMI ranges for adults to determine whether a person is at a healthy weight for their height. Use the BMI calculator to assess your fitness level and choose an appropriate exercise routine for a strong and healthy body.
